Its remain of one of the first and only 4 star hotel in the city. With a suspended swimming pool, A fully equiped gymnasium with 3 three conference room that can takes up to 300 people. The small artificial climate in the middle of the garden of the restaurant is perfect for outdoor activity. The restaurant serve both african an europeen dish. The hotel location is also a big advantage because its located in a neighborhood close to every place of the city. And there is no problem finding a parking because the hotel has is private parking for customers. Our team is well train and they can help our customer enjoy their stay in our hotel. We also offer some touristique activities to our customers, and they can also rental a car at the lobby directly.
